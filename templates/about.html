About CropMate

CropMate is your intelligent agricultural assistant — built to help farmers take the guesswork out of what to grow next. With modern technology, we bring science and simplicity together to guide planting decisions.

Using real-world data and machine learning, CropMate analyzes your soil’s Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium levels, as well as temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall, to suggest the best crop for your land.

Whether you're a traditional farmer or a first-time grower, CropMate is designed to be easy, friendly, and highly accurate. It’s farming made smarter — so you can grow more, waste less, and thrive.
